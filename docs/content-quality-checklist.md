# Content Quality Checklist

Use this before adding or importing study data.

- Each row must have `exam`, `part`, `word`, and `meaning`.
- `exam` must be one of `jcs`, `toeic`, or `custom`.
- Keep one concept per row. Do not combine multiple answers into one `word`.
- Avoid duplicate rows with the same `exam + part + word + meaning`.
- Keep `meaning` concise enough to fit in the game card.
- Put long examples in `context`, not in `meaning`.
- Verify the accepted answer exactly matches what the learner should type.
- Review new batches with at least one full playthrough on desktop and mobile.
