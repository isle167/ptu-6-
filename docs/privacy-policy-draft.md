# Privacy Policy Draft

This is a draft for review before commercial launch.

## Collected Data

- Account email and username
- Study records, wrong notes, custom words, and ranking records
- AI chat messages sent by the user
- Basic technical logs required for security and service operation

## Purpose

- Account authentication
- Study progress, review, and ranking features
- AI learning assistance
- Abuse prevention, debugging, and service improvement

## Retention

Keep user data while the account is active. Delete or anonymize data when a user requests account deletion, unless retention is legally required.

## Third Parties

Supabase is used for authentication and data storage. AI requests may be processed by an external AI provider through server-side functions.

## User Rights

Users can request access, correction, deletion, or export of their data through the service support contact.
